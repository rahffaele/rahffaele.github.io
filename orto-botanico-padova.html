Orto Botanico di Padova

2021

For the designing of the identity system of the garden we decided to enhance the secular tradition of the place, for centuries used for the cultivation of medical herbs.

This enhancement was based in the first place on the choice of the colour palette and the logo which stylistically is a reference to the world of herbal medicine.

Manifesto serie

As part of the brand identity, we designed two series of manifestos, each with different purposes.

The first serie is designed for insitutionl communications, while the second is a touristic serie to be displayed in public spaces.

Mobile application

The mobile application is a tool for the visitor to really experience the place without getting lost and intimidated by the great density of the garden. Starting from this perspective, we designed a system which combines the possibility to select an itinerary in-app with geo-localized push notifications that provide in-context information.

We also designed a concept for a plant-identifier tool through the camera.

Wayfinding system

Different signs were designed based on the historical importance of the plant. At the base of each sign some icons help the visitor to orient through the itineraries.

The wayfinding system is designed to give information to the visitor in an organized, not-crowded and not-invasive way.
